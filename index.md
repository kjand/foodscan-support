# FoodScan

**Know what's in your food.**

FoodScan is an iPhone app that checks ingredient lists against your personal rules — scan a barcode, point the camera at a label, or paste text manually.

[Support](support.md) · [Privacy Policy](privacy.md)

---

## Features

- **Barcode scan** — look up products from Open Food Facts and analyse ingredients instantly
- **Camera scan** — point at any ingredient label; text recognition runs on your device
- **Manual entry** — paste or type an ingredient list for immediate analysis
- **Personal profiles** — separate rules for each family member
- **Built-in rule packages** — gluten, lactose, added sugars, sweeteners, ultra-processed indicators
- **FoodScan Pro** — custom rule packages, unlimited avoid-list, import/export

# FoodScan

**Kjenn innholdet i maten.**

FoodScan er en iPhone-app som sjekker ingredienslister mot dine personlige regler — skann en strekkode, pek kameraet mot en etikett, eller lim inn tekst manuelt.

[Støtte](support.md) · [Personvernerklæring](privacy.md)

---

## Funksjoner

- **Strekkodesøk** — slå opp produkter fra Open Food Facts og analyser ingredienser umiddelbart
- **Kameraskann** — pek mot en ingrediensliste; tekstgjenkjenning kjøres på enheten
- **Manuell innskriving** — lim inn eller skriv en ingrediensliste for umiddelbar analyse
- **Personlige profiler** — separate regler for hvert familiemedlem
- **Innebygde regelpakker** — gluten, laktose, tilsatt sukker, søtstoffer, ultraprosessert
- **FoodScan Pro** — egendefinerte regelpakker, ubegrenset unngå-liste, import/eksport
